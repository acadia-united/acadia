Acadia United is a coalition of residents committed to responsible development that protects the people who call this community home.

What We're Advocating For

A proposed development at 117 N Gammon Rd raises serious concerns that have not been adequately addressed by the developer or by city officials. We are not opposed to thoughtful growth — we are opposed to development that puts our neighbors at risk.

Our core concerns include:

  • Traffic and pedestrian safety — the proposed access points and traffic volumes exceed what existing roads were designed to handle, endangering residents on foot and bicycle.

We are asking the city to pause the approval process, commission independent safety reviews, and hold a proper public hearing before any permits are issued.
