What We're Advocating For

A proposed development at 117 N Gammon Rd raises serious concerns that have not been adequately addressed by the developer or by city officials. We are not opposed to thoughtful growth – we are opposed to development that puts our neighbors at risk.

  • Traffic & Pedestrian Safety – Proposed access point via Acadia Ct. would increase traffic volumes fivefold on Acadia Drive, endangering residents on foot and bicycle. Traffic from Acadia Drive empties out onto major pedestrian and bike junctions at Everglade and Colony Drives, used by community members and children communiting to three schools located blocks away.
