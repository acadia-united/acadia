Acadia United is a coalition of Parkwood Hills residents committed to responsible development that protects the people who call this community home.

What We're Advocating For

A proposed development at 117 N Gammon Rd raises serious concerns which must be addressed. We are not opposed to thoughtful growth – we are opposed to development that puts our neighbors at risk.

  • Traffic & Pedestrian Safety – Proposed access point via Acadia Ct. would increase traffic volumes fivefold on Acadia Drive, endangering residents on foot and bicycle. Traffic from Acadia Drive empties out onto major pedestrian and bike junctions at Everglade and Colony Drives, used by community members and children communiting to three schools located blocks away.
  • Scale and Neighborhood Fit – Because the development site sits at a significantly higher elevation than Acadia Drive, a 3-story structure will loom over existing single-family homes like a four to five story building. We request that the height "step down" to 1- 2 stories or less on the side facing our residences to maintain consistency with our SR-C1 neighborhood.
  • Density Reduction – The proposed 96 units are too dense for a site that must transition into a quiet, suburban residential area. We want the City favor a smaller-scale project that prioritizes safety and character over maximum density.
